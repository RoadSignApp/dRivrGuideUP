RED YELLOW GREEN
YELLOW—A yellow signal light warns you that the red signal is about to appear. When you see the yellow light, you should stop, if you can do so safely. ... RED—A red signal light means STOP. RED ARROW—A red arrow means STOP until the green signal or green arrow appears. ... GREEN—A green light means GO, but you must first let any vehicles, bicycles, or pedestrians remaining in the intersection get through before you move ahead. ...
